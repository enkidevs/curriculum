# Multiplication and Division

---
## Content

The arithmetic operators for Multiplication and Division in Lua are:

|      Name      | Sign |
|:--------------:|:----:|
| Multiplication |  (*) |
|    Division    |  (/) |


Here are some examples:
```lua
-- Multiplication
result = 4 * 6  -- result = 24

-- Division
result = 10 / 2  -- result = 5
```
